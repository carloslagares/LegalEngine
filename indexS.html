Why it matters

Compliance protects your business

Regulations are tightening across Europe. Getting registered now means you can list on any platform, avoid fines, and build a business that lasts.

Fines up to €600,000

Operating without a license carries steep penalties in most European countries. In Spain, a major OTA was fined €65M for listing non-compliant properties.

Listings removed without warning

Airbnb, Booking.com, and Vrbo are now required to verify license numbers. An estimated 30% of Spanish listings are at risk of removal.

Guest reporting is mandatory

Most countries require you to report guest identity data to the police within 24 hours. Every guest, every booking — no exceptions.
